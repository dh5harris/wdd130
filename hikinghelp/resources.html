Where to go?

Trails With so many trails nearby, you don’t have to go far to find one. There are several in Land Between the Lakes (LBL) here in Kentucky. There is the Canal Loop, The North/South trail, and a few others. There are even more in southern Illinois. They are fairly easy to get to and hike.

How to get ready.

Why and how do you use water purification for when you need to refill on water? Before you go You think you can just go and get on the trail. There are some things you should consider. What items do you need to take with you, even on a short hike? Planning on where you are going is a great idea. Is it an easy or a hard trail? How long it will take you to hike the trail you are planning to hike. Don’t forget about the weather. It can change on you quickly. So be prepared.
