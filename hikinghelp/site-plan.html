Overview

Purpose

I want you to think back to the last time you went hiking or even the first time. How did it go? Did it go smoothly? What things did you forget? Were there things you wish you had known before you started or knew of any resources that could help you prepare? Even if you are wanting to go hiking for the first time or a seasoned veteran. Look no further than hikinghelp.com. Hikinghelp.com is the place to go to find the resources you need from planning, to trail conditions, to trail and gear reviews, to instructional articles and videos. We have all the help you need for hiking. Even if you are an experienced hiker, we want you to come and share your wisdom, experiences, and stories with everyone so they can learn from you. Come check us out at hikinghelp.com

Audience

Hiking Help is for hikers of all levels ranging from beginner to expert. The target age group is for all ages who have an adventurous mindset. Most of our audience will be from the tri-state region. Our beginner audience will be looking for the resources that will help them get out on the trail to have a good time and develop the love of the outdoors. For our more experienced audience will be looking for more technical resources and a place to share information for the benefit to everyone else.

Content

Home page

We love to hike. There is nothing like getting out in the great outdoors. There is something about being in nature. It can help you unwind and de-stress. We like to get out, go hiking and take in what nature is offering. We hope that you do as well. We want to help if possible. What to expect. If you are new to hiking, we are here to help you get an idea of what to expect. We have the information to help you plan to getting out and go hiking. There are plenty of trails nearby so you don’t have to go far to find one. Call to help. Those of you who are experienced and out hiking on a regular basis, we would love to have to share your experiences, thoughts, and anything else that may benefit everyone. We are waiting to hear from you.

Resources

Trails With so many trails nearby, you don’t have to go far to find one. There are several in Land Between the Lakes (LBL) here in Kentucky. There is the Canal Loop, The North/South trail, and a few others. There are even more in southern Illinois. They are fairly easy to get to and hike. “How to” Resources We have several articles and videos on how to use the gear that you might want to learn about. How and way do you want to use hiking/trekking poles? Why and how do you use water purification for when you need to refill on water? Before you go You think you can just go and get on the trail. There are some things you should consider. What items do you need to take with you, even on a short hike? Planning on where you are going is a great idea. Is it an easy or a hard trail? How long it will take you to hike the trail you are planning to hike. Don’t forget about the weather. It can change on you quickly. So be prepared.
