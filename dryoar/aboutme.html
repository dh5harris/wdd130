About Me

Daniel Harris

My name is Daniel. I am working toward a B.S. in Applied Technology. Some of things I like to do when I have time are hiking, camping, and fishing. I married my bride, Kristy, in the Nashville temple. We have two daughters together. I have two older daughters from a previous marriage.

I have been working as a career firefighter for the last 23 years. I am retiring from the fire service this year. Now that I have grown up, I am pursuing a second career in computer programming. I am grateful for the opportunity to pursue my education through BYU-Pathways and BYU-Idaho.

I have been a member of the church for all my life. My parents are both converts to the church. Since pursuing my education, one of my favorite scriptures has been James 1:5, "If any of you lack wisdom, let him ask of God, that giveth to all men liberally, and upbraideth not; and it shall be given him." It reminds me to always ask Heavenly Father for His help to learn and remember all things.
